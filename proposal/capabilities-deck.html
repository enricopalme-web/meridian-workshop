R2 — Restocking Recommendations

The capability your team has been asking for

📥

Inputs

Current stock levels, demand forecasts, and in-flight purchase orders — all already in your system. No external integrations required.

⚙️

Logic

Output

A ranked recommendation table your operations team can act on directly — or export for procurement. Budget ceiling is operator-defined, not hardcoded.
